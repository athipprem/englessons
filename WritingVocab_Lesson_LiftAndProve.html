Three ways to lift a sentence ⬆️

MoveSwap……for
1. Strong verbwalked, said, lookedmarched, whispered, glared
2. Precise wordnice, big, saddelightful, enormous, heartbroken
3. Add a comparison"ran fast""ran like a startled deer"
Before (dull)The dog ran across the garden. It was happy.
After (vivid)The dog bounded across the garden like a furry rocket, its tail whirling with joy.
Show, don't just tell
Instead of telling us "she was scared," show it: "she froze, her heart hammering." Pick one or two upgrades per sentence — you don't need all three at once.
