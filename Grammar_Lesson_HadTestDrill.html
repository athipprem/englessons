Error Review 3 — Will Ban Check

Which result clause contains a serious grammar error?

Why these three errors all come from the same place

❌ The pattern across all three:

Q11 — Had Test not applied: "had a larger budget" = had + noun = Second, not Third. You chose a correct sentence instead of the mismatch.

Q12 — Time signal missed: "today" = present result = Mixed. "Would have been" places the result in the past → Third, which ignores "today".

Will Ban — "will" in a result clause = always wrong for Second, Third, and Mixed conditionals.

Had Test → Will Ban → Mismatch Check
In that order, every grammar question.

⚠ The Had Test — the single most important rule
Look at the word immediately after "had" in the if-clause:

"had won" → "won" is a past participle → past perfect → Third or Mixed
"had a bigger room" → "a bigger room" is a noun phrase → simple past of "have" → Second
"had better equipment" → "better" is an adjective → simple past of "have" → Second
