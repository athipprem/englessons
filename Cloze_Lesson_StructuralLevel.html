The common thread in all three errors

These three errors look different on the surface — wrong structural level, wrong structural level again, and a distractor. But they all come from the same missed step.

❌ What was skipped:

Before testing function, you must check structural level. A full stop before the gap eliminates every clause-joiner from consideration. A comma eliminates every sentence-starter. "Despite" is eliminated even earlier — it cannot function as a standalone connector at all.

Structure first. Function second.
Never the other way round.

⚠ The most common mistake
"While" and "meanwhile" have the same function (TIME) but different structural levels. "While" = clause-joiner (sits mid-sentence). "Meanwhile" = sentence-starter (follows a full stop). Getting the function right but the level wrong still costs you the mark.
