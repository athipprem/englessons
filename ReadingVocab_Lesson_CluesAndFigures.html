Part 1 · Reading — the four figures 🎨

FigureWhat it doesExample
Similecompares with like / as"as heavy as bricks"
Metaphorsays one thing IS another"the library was a treasure chest"
Personificationa thing does a human action"the fog crept silently"
Idioma saying that doesn't mean its words"a bolt from the blue"
The two reader questions

1. Which figure is it?   2. What feeling does it create? (danger, calm, sadness, joy…)

From the Milestone
"Like moving a family grave" showed deep loss — not real graves. Always read the figure for its feeling.
